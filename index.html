WHAT THIS IS

INTERRUPT is a short, structured session.

You will:

  • identify the pattern
  • break it once in real time
  • replace it with a new action
  • lock that action in

This is not about thinking.
It’s about doing something different, immediately.

HOW THIS WORKS

Most behaviours follow a loop:

  • Something triggers it → You act → You feel brief relief → There’s a cost later

In this session, you will step in before the action takes over and change what happens next.

⚠️ IMPORTANT RULE

You have about 3 seconds to act.

When the trigger happens:

  • If you hesitate → you’ll default to the old behaviour
  • If you act → you interrupt the pattern

This only works if you move quickly.

WHAT YOU’LL DO INSIDE

Capture the pattern
What started it, what you did, and what it cost you

Break it down
See the pattern clearly so it becomes controllable

Choose a new action
Something small, physical, and easy to do under pressure

Use it in real time
When the trigger happens, act within seconds

Record what happened
This locks in the change

IMPORTANT CONSTRAINTS

To make this work:

  • Keep your replacement action simple (under 30 seconds)
  • Make it physical (not just thinking)
  • Don’t wait until you “feel ready”
  • Don’t overthink your answers

If it feels too complicated, it won’t work.
Simpler is stronger.
